Overview

PixelToPath is a free image-to-SVG conversion tool operated by an individual based in France. This policy describes what data is collected when you use this service and how it is handled.

Contact: pixel-to-path.reexamine129@simplelogin.com

📦

Data collected

When you convert an image, the following is recorded on our server:

Data point Detail
IP hash Non-reversible hash - raw IP never stored
Image metadata Dimensions and format (e.g. 800×600 PNG)
Color mode The conversion setting you selected
Processing time Duration of the conversion in ms
Uploaded file Stored temporarily - deleted after 24 hours
🔒

Your raw IP address is never stored in any file or exposed publicly. Only a one-way hash is retained.

🌍

Geolocation

We derive an approximate country and city from your IP address using a local copy of the MaxMind GeoLite2 database. This lookup is performed entirely on our server.

✅

Your IP address is never sent to any third party for geolocation purposes.

📊

Analytics

We use a self-hosted instance of Matomo Analytics with the following settings:

  • Cookies disabled
  • IP anonymization enabled
  • Data stored on our own infrastructure only
📡

No analytics data leaves our infrastructure. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking service.

🚫

What we don't do

🚫

Sell or share your data with third parties

🍪

Use cookies of any kind

📢

Display advertising

🔍

Process your images for any purpose other than the conversion you requested
